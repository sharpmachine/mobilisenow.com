Schools

The aim of these  schools  is to mobilise and equip you to be an active member of  the Body, knowing your place and calling and confidently proclaiming the gospel in your unique way wherever you go.  This includes identifying your unique gifts and  your place in the Body. It also involves  activating, exercising and stirring up the gifts, callings and God given desires within you.  To take your place as a last day saint, you must also be aware of the times, you must have an Issachar anointing and be able to discern the times.  Mobilise offers the following schools at present. All schools are tailored to the specific needs of each group.

Quick Overview of Schools:

ENCOUNTER

This event, usually conducted over a weekend, is about pressing in, pressing in, pressing in and pressing in to enjoy being with God. It’s about being refreshed in His Presence and connecting with Him intimately.  Learn More…

BREAKOUT

Sometimes it’s necessary to do a BIG PUSH in prayer, warfare and worship to remove obstacles and open up areas in the spirit realm. Results nearly always follow like increased salvations, increased hunger for God or changed lives and growth in local churches.The ministry of the prophet will recognise what needs to be done in the spirit and lead us into prayer, worship, declaration or action.  Learn More…

MOBILISE THE PROPHETIC

A New Covenant reality is that every believer can hear from God and has free access to His throne. It is a amazing but true fact that God wants to communicate with each one of us, all the time.  In this school a  strong biblical foundation for prophecy and the gifts of the Spirit  is laid and then  participants are activated  in various aspects of  prophecy.  Learn More…

MOBILISE PRAYER AND INTERCESSION

The church needs to engage in the ministry of Jesus. Right now He ever lives to intercede and pray for the saints. This ministry will train believers to flow in prayer with God the Holy Spirit. There are rivers of prayer and different kinds of prayers.The church needs to learn to release these rivers both individually and corporately.  Learn More…

MOBILISE SCHOOL OF HEALING AND DELIVERANCE

Is there sickness in Heaven? What is God’s will for healing? Jesus is the same yesterday, today and forever and He still heals today. His love and power to heal are available to us today. This school teaches how to pray for the sick and to cast out demons.  Learn More…

DREAM INTERPRETATION WORKSHOP

God has always communicated with us through dreams and visions and He still does so today. In fact there seems to be a significant and powerful anointing that God has released on the ability to interpret dreams. It has certainly been one of our most successful tools to reach both believers and unbelievers. It is a wonderful evangelistic tool. This workshop  lays a strong biblical foundation and also includes  specific activations and excercises to develop your ability to interpret dreams. Learn More…

DISCOVERING YOUR GIFTS

God has wired each one of us in a  very specific way and ‘we are His workmanship created for good works.’  He has given each one of us natural gifts and talents as well as spiritual gifts and talents. Some, He has called as ‘coaches’ in the Kingdom. All of us should be able to identify at least some of our gifts so that we can take our place with confidence in the Body. This workshop will help you to explore and identify your gifts.  Learn More…

If you are interested in having a Mobilise Now  team do a school at your church or event, please contact us.
