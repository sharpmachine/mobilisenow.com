Giving

Join our support team…become a monthly partner.

We appreciate any support you feel lead to give…make a one-time donation.

Jesus said in the gospel of John chapter 7 v 38 that “He who believes in Me, as scripture has said, from his innermost being will flow springs and rivers of living water”. He was speaking here of the Spirit. Today a huge percentage of the body of Christ is inactive and we need your help to enable us to mobilise them. They don’t know what these rivers are, or how to activate and use them. Our task is to enable as many Christians as we possibly can to learn to activate and release the rivers of God from their innermost beings. This will raise the influence of the gospel and the Holy Spirit in the places wherever they are.

Your generous giving is used to:

  • Produce resources designed to train, educate, motivate and equip.
  • Organise workshops, schools and training seminars.
  • Promote the workshops, schools, seminars and resources.
  • Train team members for end time mobilisation of the body of Christ.
  • Fund our Kingdom Projects.

We appreciate your help to get this job done!
